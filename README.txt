ULTRA GOD MODE Macro Tracker (Home Screen App)
=================================================

This is a PWA (Progressive Web App). It works offline AFTER the first load.

Install on iPhone (Safari):
1) Upload these files to a HTTPS website (GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel).
2) Open the site in Safari.
3) Share -> Add to Home Screen.

Offline mode:
- After first load, it runs offline (service worker caches files).
- Your entries and goals are saved on your phone (localStorage).

Barcode scan:
- Tap Scan barcode (requires camera permission).
- Captures the barcode and places it into the Food name field.

Export:
- Tap Export CSV to download all entries as a CSV file.
